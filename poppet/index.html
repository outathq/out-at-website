What makes it different

Community-led, not package-led.

Friendly by design

We start with smaller touchpoints, clear expectations and groups that feel welcoming to singles, couples and friends.

Plans with breathing room

Expect anchor moments like welcome drinks, local tips and group chats, with freedom to opt in or peel off.

Rooted in the UK

Out At begins with UK socials and travellers, then builds towards European weekends and future cruises.

The feeling

Less “who am I going with?” More “see you there.”

The best trips often come down to the people around the table, the easy laugh on the walk back, and the comfort of knowing someone will save you a seat. That is the part Out At is here to build.
